Bachelor in Computer Technology

The BCT is a four-year degree program thath focuses on the practical aspects of the science of computing. The Program develops core skills in multimedia technology, web mobile dvelopment and networks system administration. Furthermore, it prepares learners with supervisory and management skills. The curriculum is derived from the BS Industrial Technology major in Computer Technology with CHED Certificate of Program Compliance (COPC) No. 31, series of 2021.

Program Objectives:

The program prepares learners with the practical skills in multimedia technology, web/mobile development, and network/systems administration, and supervisorial skills to mange an organizarion in these area of competencies, engage in a startup business or pursue a teaching career.

Specific objectives:

The learner should be able to:

  1. Demonstrate understanding of the importance of being an ethical computing professional when making work-related decisions.
  2. Develop products or processess by applying their knowledge in computer or network systems, and development of productivity tools.
  3. Participate as an active member of a development/service team with leadership roles when appropriate.
  4. Apply communication skills effectively to promote ideas, goals, processes or products.
  5. Make positive contributions to the communitywith the skills and abilities learned in the Program.
  6. Pursue lifelong learning for personal and professional growth through varied experiential and filed-based opportunities.
